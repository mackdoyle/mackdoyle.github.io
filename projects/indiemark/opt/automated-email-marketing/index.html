Email Automation

Turning Email Marketing Automation into ROI

As an experienced email agency, we know the rewards and risks associated with marketing automation. Let us get you started or ensure that you're making the most of today's powerful marketing automation technologies.


Grow Sales


By combining triggered email marketing with your CRM system or e-commerce platform we can create personalized emails that are sent at just the right time to alert users about such things as Shopping Cart Abandonment. These personal messages build strong relationships and allow for more relevant offers


Convert Leads


For B2B and Online Retail companies, growth is all about wisely managing your sales process and delivering a repeatable experience for customers. Through email marketing automation you'll be able to identify hot leads who are ready to buy, without lifting a finger.


Save Time


To a growing small business, time is especially valuable. Automated email marketing helps you save time by automatically keeping all of your systems connected, which eliminates humdrum tasks and puts many of your sales and marketing processes on autopilot. With email automation, you can forget about the small stuff and focus on more important aspects of your business.


Not only will we automate your email program, but we offer a full array of services to help you make the most of this lucrative marketing channel.
