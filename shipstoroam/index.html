About

Ships to Roam is a professional Folk N' Roll band in McMinnville, Oregon, currently booking regional gigs.
Ships to Roam was born in 2005 as the solo project of singer/songwriter Jerome Blankenship. Funeral Songs & Lullabies, StR's first album was released in 2006 to critical acc laim.
